1. LOCATION SELECTION: NEOM vs. King Abdullah Economic City
   
   RECOMMENDED: NEOM (The Line)
   ✓ 0% corporate tax (vs. 5-10% in KAEC)
   ✓ 35% CAPEX reimbursement (vs. 30%)
   ✓ 100% renewable energy allocation
   ✓ Direct PIF co-investment access
   ✓ Regulatory sandbox for AI governance
   
   CONTINGENCY: King Abdullah Economic City
   ✓ Immediate operational readiness (no construction risk)
   ✓ Established connectivity (operational since 2005)
   ✓ Port access (Jeddah Islamic Port integration)
   ⚠ Slightly lower incentives (still 30% CAPEX)

2. REGULATORY FAST-TRACK: Single-window licensing via MISA
   → Investment license: 2-4 weeks (vs. 6-12 months regional average)
   → 100% foreign ownership (no local partner required)
   → Premium Residency: Express visa processing for executives

3. GOVERNMENT PARTNERSHIP: Vision 2030 anchor customer strategy
   → Saudi Data & AI Authority (SDAIA): Guaranteed contracts
   → Public Investment Fund (PIF): Co-investment opportunities
   → Ministry contracts: 30-40% capacity pre-commitment

4. LABOR FLEXIBILITY: Progressive Saudization vs. immediate quotas
   → Years 1-5: 0% Saudi workforce requirement
   → Years 6-10: 15% requirement (vs. 25-30% immediate in other sectors)
   → Salary subsidies: 15% of Saudi technical expert salaries
